# Registry by Tenra Handoffs

Registry by Tenra stays unique as the rental operating desk. Customers, units, rentals, receivables, imports, and documents are a complete domain.

## Produces

- `tenra-registry.ledger-export.v1` for Ledger receivable import.
- `tenra-registry.assembly-document-request.v1` for Assembly content/document drafting.

## Consumes

- No required handoffs for core rental/sales operation.

Registry should not grow bookkeeping, content generation, suite catalog, or policy enforcement beyond its rental/sales domain. Ledger and Assembly stay optional, explicit export paths.
